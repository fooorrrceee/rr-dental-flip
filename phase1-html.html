Frequently asked questions

What is dentistry?

Dentistry focuses on the health of your teeth, gums, and mouth, helping prevent and treat common problems.

What is a cavity?

A cavity is a damaged area in a tooth caused by decay, often treated with a filling.

When should I bring my child for a first visit?

A common guideline is to schedule a visit by the first birthday or within six months after the first tooth appears.
